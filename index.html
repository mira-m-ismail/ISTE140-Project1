Welcome to the Samarkand Travel Guide, your starting point to explore one of Uzbekistan's most historic and culturally rich cities. Samarkand is a place where stunning Islamic architecture meets bustling bazaars and centuries of history. This guide will help you discover the best attractions, foods, and cultural experiences.

From ancient madrasas to traditional markets, Samarkand has something for everyone. Visit Registan square, admire the intricate mosaics at Shah-i-Zinda, or explore the Gur-e-Amir mausoleum. Don't forget to try local dishes like plov and shashlik, and immerse yourself in the vibrant culture of this historic city.

Top 3 Thing To Pack

  1. Lightweight clothes
  2. Sunscreen & sunglasses
  3. Power adapter
