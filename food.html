Samarkand Food Scene

Samarkand's food scene reflects its rich history and cultural diversity. From street snacks to traditional dishes, every meal is a culinary experience.

Try Plov, a spiced rice dish, or enjoy Samsa, flaky stuffed pastries. Shurpa, a hearty soup, is perfect for a warm meal, while adventurous travelers can sample local desserts and street treats.

Dish Prices (UZS)
Dish Average Price (UZS)
Plov 30,000-50,000
Samsa 5,000-10,000
Shurpa 15,000-25,000
