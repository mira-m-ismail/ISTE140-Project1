Famous Landmarks

Samarkand offers world-famous landmarks and unique experiences. Its blend of historic architecture and vibrant culture makes it a destination like no other.

Visit Registan for its stunning madrasas, admire the tomb of Timur at Gur-e-Amir, or stroll through Shah-i-Zinda's mausoleum complex. For a more traditional experience, explore local bazaars filled with spices, textiles, and handcrafted souvenirs.
